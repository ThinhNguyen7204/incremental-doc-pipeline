Answer:
To add a YouTube video to OptiSigns, follow these steps:

*   Log on to your OptiSigns portal at app.optisigns.com.
*   Go to Files/Assets, click on "App," and then select "YouTube."
*   Enter a **Name** for your YouTube video.
*   Paste the actual **URL** of your YouTube video. For YouTube Shorts, change the `/shorts/` part of the URL to `/embed/`.
*   Click Save. You can then assign the newly created YouTube asset to your screen.
